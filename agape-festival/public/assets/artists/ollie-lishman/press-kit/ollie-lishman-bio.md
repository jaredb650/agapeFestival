Ollie Lishman is a distinguished DJ and Producer hailing from Naarm/Melbourne Australia,
whose meteoric rise in the electronic music scene is propelled by his dynamic fusion of trance,
techno, and iconic grooves, captivating dance floors worldwide. 

Drawing inspiration from the pulsating beats of 90s techno, hard house, and trance, Ollie's 
productions have garnered widespread acclaim, earning him international recognition from esteemed
figures such as Trym,Narciss, Marlon Hoffstadt, DJ Heartstring, among others. His tracks have 
become fixtures in the global arena, featured in renowned Boiler Rooms and festivals. Following
a triumphant debut summer in Europe and back home in Australia in 2023/24, Ollie returns to Europe this summer,
poised for an even greater 2024 abroad. Ollie's artistic journey is characterized by sonic
innovation, seamlessly bridging the realms of hard house, techno intensity, and trance euphoria.
His oeuvre stands as a testament to his meticulous craftsmanship and unwavering dedication to
the electronic music landscape.
